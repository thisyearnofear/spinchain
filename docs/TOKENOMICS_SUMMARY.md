# SPIN Tokenomics: Executive Summary

## The Core Insight

**Problem**: Most "X-to-earn" tokens fail because there's no sustainable demand. Users earn tokens, immediately sell them, and the price collapses.

**Solution**: Every class payment on SpinChain automatically buys and burns SPIN. The more popular the platform, the more valuable SPIN becomes. Users hold SPIN not for speculation, but for real discounts and access.

---

## The "SpinCycle" in 30 Seconds

1. **User pays $25** for a spin class (in USDC/ETH/SUI)
2. **15% ($3.75)** goes to treasury
3. **Treasury swaps** $3.75 → SPIN on DEX
4. **20% of SPIN is burned** (deflation)
5. **80% funds rewards** + staking
6. **SPIN price rises** from buy pressure
7. **Users hold SPIN** for 5-25% class discounts
8. **More users join** → [Cycle repeats]

**Result**: Sustainable, demand-driven tokenomics.

---

## Three Key Mechanisms

### 1. Automatic Buybacks (The Engine)

Every single class payment creates SPIN demand. No relying on speculation.

| Daily Classes | Monthly Buyback | Annual Buyback |
|---------------|-----------------|----------------|
| 10 | $1,125 | $13,500 |
| 100 | $11,250 | $135,000 |
| 1,000 | $112,500 | $1,350,000 |
| 10,000 | $1,125,000 | $13,500,000 |

At scale, this creates **massive, sustained buy pressure**.

### 2. Progressive Benefits (The Magnet)

Users hold SPIN because it saves them money. Not speculation—utility.

| Tier | Hold | Discount | Annual Savings* |
|------|------|----------|-----------------|
| Spinner | 100 SPIN | 5% | $60 |
| Cyclist | 500 SPIN | 10% | $120 |
| Peloton | 2,000 SPIN | 15% | $180 |
| Century | 10,000 SPIN | 25% | $300 |

*Assumes $100/month class spend

**ROI**: Even at $0.05/SPIN, a Cyclist saves $120/year on a $25 investment = **480% ROI**.

### 3. Instructor Challenges (The Flywheel)

Instructors create competitions that drive engagement and SPIN demand.

**Example Challenge**:
- Prize: 1,000 SPIN
- Entry: 50 SPIN per rider
- 20 riders = 1,000 SPIN collected
- Winner gets 800 SPIN
- Platform burns 100 SPIN
- **Result**: Zero cost to instructor, massive engagement, SPIN demand

## The Bottom Line

**SPIN is designed to be valuable because the platform is valuable.**

Not the other way around. Every time someone takes a spin class, SPIN becomes scarcer. Every time someone wants a discount, they buy SPIN. Every time an instructor wants engagement, they create SPIN demand.

It's a **virtuous cycle** that rewards early adopters, incentivizes long-term holding, and creates sustainable value for everyone in the ecosystem.
